Finding your ride with us: FAQs

Are there any extra fees added to the price advertised? +
We account for any extra fees including insurance, local taxes and airport surcharges in the price shown when you search. Many of our insurance partners offer an excess refund option, which may prove cheaper than the rental companies excess waiver offered at the rental counter. Additional drivers or child seats can be purchased when you pick the car up.
Can I return my hire car to a different location? +
Yes, some car hire providers like Hertz and Europcar allow you to pick up a car in one location and drop it off in another. Simply select Return car to different location when you search with us. Note that you could be charged a one-way hire fee.
Do I need a credit card to rent a car? +
Although it might be smoother and faster to hire a car with a credit card, these days most major providers like Sixt, Avis, Budget, Hertz will allow you to hire car with a debit card if you have a certain amount of cash on it.
Can I hire a van? +
Of course! Whether you need to move furniture from one side of Rawalpindi to another, or you're organising a trip for up to 12 people, you can search for vans and filter by capacity or space for suitcases.
What kind of vehicle can I hire? +
We scan the internet for all kinds of vehicle rental deals, so you'll find everything from cheap and cheerful hatchbacks, to exotic sports cars on Skyscanner. You can even find deals on motor homes or passenger vans, too.
